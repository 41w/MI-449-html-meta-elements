MSU Biochemistry and Molecular Biology Club

The Biochemistry and Molecular Biology Club is a student run organization dedicated to advancing the interests of BMB undergraduates. Club activities are organized to encourage students in research, academics, internship opportunities, graduate school applications, career opportunities and fun. Biochemistry club events are open to all interested undergraduates, not just BMB majors.

The BMB Club meets twice a month on Thursdays at 6:00pm in room 101 Biochemistry Building.
